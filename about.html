Our Story

For nearly two decades, Antonio Restaurant has been serving authentic Italian cuisine with passion, tradition, and a modern twist.

Our Journey

From a small family kitchen to a beloved neighborhood institution

A Family Legacy

Founded in 2005 by Antonio and Maria Rossi, our restaurant began as a small dream in the heart of Little Italy. With recipes passed down through generations, we set out to share the authentic flavors of our Italian heritage with New York City.

What started as a 30-seat trattoria has grown into a 150-seat restaurant, but our commitment to quality, family, and tradition has never wavered. Every dish we serve tells a story of our journey from the hills of Tuscany to the streets of New York.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while embracing innovation, creating a dining experience that blends the comfort of tradition with the excitement of modern Italian cuisine.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Passion

We pour our hearts into every dish, ensuring that each meal is prepared with the same love and care as if we were serving our own family.

Quality

We source only the finest ingredients, working with local farmers and Italian importers to bring you authentic flavors and exceptional quality.

Community

We believe in building relationships with our guests and neighbors, creating a warm, welcoming environment where everyone feels like family.

Tradition

We honor time-tested recipes and cooking methods while adding our own creative touches to keep Italian cuisine vibrant and exciting.

Excellence

We strive for perfection in every aspect of our restaurant, from food preparation to service, creating memorable experiences for all our guests.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices, from reducing food waste to supporting sustainable farming and fishing methods.
